### The Idea

You know meditation would help. But sitting in silence feels impossible when your mind won't stop racing. Aura meets you where you are — guided sessions for stress, sleep, and focus. Ambient sounds when you need background calm. Sleep stories when counting sheep isn't cutting it.

---

### What It Does

| | |
|---|---|
| **Guided Meditations** | Sessions for stress, sleep, focus & more |
| **Ambient Sounds** | Nature sounds and calming music |
| **Sleep Stories** | Drift off with soothing narratives |
| **Mood Tracking** | Monitor your emotional well-being |
| **Timer** | Unguided meditation with bells |
| **Streaks** | Build consistent mindfulness habits |

### Design

Calming dark theme with purple (`#A855F7`) and blue (`#6366F1`) gradients — soft rounded cards with blur effects, animated breathing circles, waveform audio visualizations, and calming typography.
